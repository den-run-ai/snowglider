Race a snowman down a procedurally generated mountain: hit every checkpoint, carve past trees, launch off jumps — and outrun the avalanche chasing you down the slope. Beat the clock and your own best time for the fastest run!

Controls

← A Turn Left
→ D Turn Right
↑ W Accelerate
↓ S Brake
Space Jump
V Toggle Chase View

⛷️ Ski Techniques

←→ Parallel turn — steer to brush the skis & scrub speed for a tighter turn
←→ hold Carve — hold a smooth turn: skis edge in, lean hard, hold speed (wide arc)
↓S Snowplow / pizza — wedge the tips together: tap to slow, hold to stop (too steep = only slows)
↑W Tuck — straight-line (no steering) for maximum speed; fold into a low aero crouch
Space+←→ Hop turn — quick pivot for tight, steep terrain

📱 On touch devices: tap the left/right of the screen to steer, top/bottom for speed, and the center to jump.

🔐 Optional: sign in (top‑right) to save your best time to the global leaderboard.

About SnowGlider

SnowGlider is a 3D skiing game where you guide a snowman down a procedurally generated backcountry mountain.

Reach the finish as fast as you can: pass through the checkpoint gates, watch your split times, and beat the fastest run you've recorded.

Keep an eye uphill — once you've descended far enough, an avalanche of tumbling snow boulders gives chase. Get caught and it's game over.

Sign in with Google to save your best time to the global leaderboard. Made with Three.js and a lot of love for winter sports.

Press Enter or Space to start the game
